Horizon Zero Dawn

Horizon Zero Dawn – Most interesting and enjoyable game played.

Horizon Zero Dawn is an action role-playing game developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment.
It was released for the PlayStation 4 in early 2017.
The plot follows Aloy, a hunter in a world overrun by machines, who sets out to uncover her past.
The player uses ranged weapons, a spear, and stealth to combat mechanical creatures and other enemy forces.
A skill tree provides the player with new abilities and bonuses.
The player can explore the open world to discover locations and take on side quests.

Game Critic ratings:
IGN – 9.3/10.
GamesRadar – 4.5/5.
Trusted Reviews – 4.5/5.
