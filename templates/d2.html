Destiny 2

Destiny 2 – My favorite FPS (First Person Shooter) and grinding game

Destiny 2 is an online-only multiplayer first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie.
It was released for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on September 6, 2017, followed by a Microsoft Windows version the following month.
The game was published by Activision until early 2019, when Bungie acquired the publishing rights to the franchise.
Set in a "mythic science fiction" world, the game features a multiplayer "shared-world" environment with elements of role-playing games.

Game critics ratings:
IGN – 8.5/10.
Steam – 9/10.
PC Gamer – 76%.
