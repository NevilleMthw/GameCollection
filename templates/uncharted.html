Uncharted 4: Thief's End

Uncharted 4: Thief’s End – My first PS4 game.

Uncharted 4: A Thief's End is a 2016 action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment.
It is the fourth main entry in the Uncharted series.
A Thief's End is played from a third-person perspective; incorporating several platformer elements to advance the narrative.
Players use firearms, melee combat, and stealth to combat hostile enemies as well as solving puzzles.
The game world is navigated by driving vehicles and by using a grappling hook.
In the online multiplayer mode, up to ten players can engage in co-operative and competitive game modes.

Game critic ratings:
IGN – 9/10.
Metacritic – 93%.
GameSpot – 10/10.
